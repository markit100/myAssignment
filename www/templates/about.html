Calorie Counter

What we can do for you!

Calories are defined as little creatures that live in your wardrobe and sew your clothes tighter every night! They need food calories to have the energy to do all of this damage and food calories are the amount of energy needed to raise the temperature of one kilogram of water by one degree Celsius. The large calorie is thus equal to 1000 small calories or one kilocalorie.
